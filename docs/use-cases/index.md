# Use Cases

Find out what SlideShow can be used for.

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   __Basic advertisement display__

    ---

    - Show promotional content, product highlights, and announcements to customers in real time.
    - Upload images and videos easily through the integrated web interface or via USB flash drive — perfect for quick content updates.
    - Enrich your campaigns with dynamic add‑ons such as current weather, time, live news tickers, and RSS feeds.

-   __Interactive kiosk__

    ---

    - Create touch‑enabled interfaces or trigger actions based on person detection for more engaging public installations.
    - Display websites with optional domain restrictions for safe, focused browsing.
    - Add an automatic screensaver for idle periods.
    - Lock devices into kiosk mode for tamper‑proof operation.

-   __Music & Audio Player__

    ---

    - Run SlideShow in headless audio‑only mode using 3.5 mm audio output or SPDIF optical audio.
    - Schedule different music or audio content throughout the day — ideal for retail, hospitality, or events.
    - Stream internet radio or external audio streams with ease.

-   __Dashboard viewer__

    ---

    - Display real‑time dashboards from tools like Zabbix, Grafana, or other web‑based monitoring systems.
    - Enable automatic login and periodic refresh for unattended status boards and operations centers.

-   __Outdoor LED board__

    ---

    - Drive LED screens directly using built‑in support for NovaStar LED controllers — no extra hardware required.
    - Adjust layouts freely to match any custom resolution or panel configuration.

-   __Video Wall__

    ---

    - Synchronize playback across multiple devices for seamless multi‑screen video walls, supporting up to 20 screens.
    - Achieve professional results without expensive proprietary video‑wall hardware.

-   __TV Overlay__

    ---

    - Use HDMI input (on supported devices) to overlay content on top of a live TV signal.
    - Add semi‑transparent graphics, announcements, or alerts without interrupting the underlying broadcast.

</div>
